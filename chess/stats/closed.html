This website is a live rating site for the highest rated Colorado chess players in three categories. This website will be updated weekly, usually on a weekday in order to include the past weekend's tournament results. Superlatives are also included on this site, so be sure to vote!

The source of this data comes from the USCF, from this link.

Players who haven't played a standard tournament within 1 year of the update date will not be included on the list.

The tournament might not reflect the rating and ranking changes shown, this is because the rating and ranking changes are the changes throughout the entire month, while the tournament is the most recent tournament the player played in.
